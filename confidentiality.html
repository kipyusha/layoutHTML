Privacy Policy of NexaFlow

Effective Date: 2025-10-07

Welcome to the privacy policy page of NexaFlow. Our commitment to protecting your privacy is paramount. This document outlines the types of personal information we gather from users, how we use, protect, and disclose this information, and your rights regarding your personal data. Serving our customers in Canada, we adhere to all relevant laws and regulations concerning privacy and personal data protection.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, and physical address. This information is collected when you visit our website, engage with our services, or communicate with us.

We may also collect non-identifiable information using cookies and similar technologies. This helps us understand your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is primarily used to operate and facilitate the services we provide. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Enhance your user experience on our website.
  • Communicate with you regarding our services and support.
  • Process transactions and send confirmations.
  • Improve our products and services based on your feedback.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor the activity on our website and store certain information. These technologies help us improve our service and provide you with customized content. If you wish to disable cookies, you can do so through your browser settings. However, this may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We value your privacy and do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

We may also disclose your information when required by law or to protect our or others' rights, property, or safety.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to safeguard your personal information. These include encryption, firewalls, and access controls. Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, hence we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights

As a resident of Canada, you have specific rights regarding your personal data under applicable privacy laws, including the right to access, correction, deletion, and objection to data processing. To exercise your rights, please contact us at support@nexaflow-ca.com.

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you and to ask for corrections if any information is inaccurate or outdated.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When personal data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
